Meet Your Executive Board

Political Science Student Association — 2026–2027

The PSSA Executive Board is composed of dedicated Political Science scholars committed to serving the student body, building community, and creating meaningful opportunities for growth—academically, professionally, and civically.

◆ 2026–2027 Leadership ◆
Founding President

Amany Hilario

Amany Hilario is the Founding President of the Political Science Student Association at Florida A&M University, where he works to build a space for civic engagement, meaningful dialogue, and student development. In this role, he focuses on connecting academic learning to real-world issues and encouraging students to actively participate in political and community life on and off campus. A first-generation college student and aspiring attorney, Amany is also the Founder of Serve First Inc., a nonprofit dedicated to connecting students and community members with resources, mentorship, and opportunities that support long-term success. His leadership journey began in TRIO Upward Bound, a program that shaped his commitment to supporting first-generation and underrepresented students. He has since served as Student Government President, a statewide representative for the Florida College System Student Government Association, and a member of the Tallahassee Chamber of Commerce Board of Directors. Amany leads with intentionality and discipline, grounded in Jeremiah 29:11: “For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
