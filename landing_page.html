Welcome

Welcome, Political Science Majors and Minors!

This course shell is dedicated to you. It was created to help streamline information from the department and political science faculty directly to students, as well as provide a method for you to communicate with faculty and peers.

The goal of this course shell is to expose you to opportunities, resources, important timelines, program requirements, and key deadlines that can benefit your academic and professional journey. Through this platform, you will receive announcements from the department and stay informed about activities and initiatives within the discipline.

This course will also serve as a hub for department-supported student organizations such as the Political Science Student Association and others.

Again, welcome, and thank you for your engagement.
